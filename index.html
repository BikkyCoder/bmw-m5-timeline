BMW M5 Price in India | All Models 2025

BMW M5 All Models Price in India

The BMW M5 is one of the most coveted performance sedans globally, and India is no exception. Here's a comprehensive guide to BMW M5 pricing across all generations available in the Indian market:

BMW M5 F90 Price in India

The BMW M5 F90 (2017-2023) is currently the most popular model in India. The F90 M5 price in India ranges from ₹1.5 Crores to ₹2 Crores on-road. The M5 F90 Competition variant costs ₹1.7-1.8 Crores. Pre-owned F90 M5 models are available from ₹1.2 Crores onwards depending on year, mileage, and condition. Key specs: 625 HP, 0-100 km/h in 3.8 seconds, top speed 250 km/h, M xDrive AWD.

BMW M5 G90 Price in India 2025

The latest BMW M5 G90 (2024-present) is the newest hybrid M5 available in India. G90 M5 price in India is approximately ₹2.5-3 Crores on-road. It features the most powerful engine ever: 735 HP hybrid powertrain, 0-100 km/h in just 2.8 seconds, top speed 250 km/h (electronically limited), hybrid efficiency with 10-12 km/l mileage. M xDrive standard on all variants.

BMW M5 CS Price & Specifications

The exclusive BMW M5 CS is a limited edition with only 8,000 units produced worldwide. M5 CS price in India: ₹2-2.5 Crores. Specifications include 635 HP, 0-100 km/h in 3.0 seconds, carbon fiber lightweight construction, M carbon brakes, exclusive design elements.

BMW M5 E39 Price & Legacy

The legendary BMW M5 E39 (1998-2003) is considered one of the greatest M5s ever. E39 M5 price in India (pre-owned/vintage): ₹50 Lakhs to ₹1 Crore depending on condition and mileage. Features: 400 HP naturally aspirated V8, 4.7s 0-100 km/h, 250 km/h top speed, timeless design.

F90 M5 (2017-2023): ₹1.5-2 Crores. 625 HP, M xDrive AWD revolution.

G90 M5 (2024-Present): ₹2.5-3 Crores. 735 HP hybrid, most powerful M5 ever.

BMW M5 0-60 Times & Top Speed

0-100 km/h (0-60 mph) acceleration times across generations: E28 M5 (6.5s), E39 M5 (4.7s), F10 M5 (3.9s), F90 M5 (3.8s), G90 M5 (2.8s fastest). Top speed comparison: E28 210 km/h, E39 250 km/h, E60 250 km/h, F10 305 km/h (delimited), F90 250 km/h, G90 250 km/h. G90 M5 offers fastest acceleration of any M5 ever.

BMW M5 Mileage & Fuel Efficiency

BMW M5 mileage figures (ARAI combined): E39 M5 7-9 km/l, F10 M5 7-8 km/l, F90 M5 8-9 km/l, G90 M5 hybrid 10-12 km/l. Fuel efficiency depends on driving conditions and style. Highway driving typically achieves 1-2 km/l better than city driving. G90 M5 hybrid offers best fuel economy due to electric motor assistance.

BMW M5 Horsepower Evolution

Horsepower progression shows the dramatic evolution: E28 M5 286 HP → E34 M5 340 HP → E39 M5 400 HP → E60 M5 500 HP → F10 M5 560 HP → F90 M5 625 HP → G90 M5 735 HP. This 449 HP increase (157% growth) demonstrates BMW's commitment to continuous performance improvement across 40 years.
