About BMW M5 Heritage

Dedicated to Preserving M5 Excellence

Founded by passionate BMW enthusiasts and automotive journalists, BMW M5 Heritage is committed to documenting and preserving the rich history of BMW's legendary M5 super sedan. Our mission is to provide enthusiasts, owners, and researchers with the most comprehensive and accurate information about every M5 generation.

Our Mission

Document Excellence

Meticulously research and document every aspect of the M5's evolution, from technical specifications to historical significance.

Preserve History

Maintain a comprehensive digital archive of M5 history, ensuring future generations understand its impact on automotive culture.

Connect Enthusiasts

Build a global community of M5 enthusiasts, facilitating knowledge sharing and passion for BMW's legendary super sedan.

Our Expertise

With over 50 years of combined experience in automotive journalism and BMW expertise, our team brings unparalleled knowledge to every article and specification. We maintain direct relationships with BMW M GmbH, allowing us to provide accurate, detailed information about every M5 generation.

  • Comprehensive technical knowledge of all M5 generations
  • Direct access to BMW M archives and historical documents
  • Relationships with key M5 development engineers
  • Expert analysis of performance data and specifications
  • Deep understanding of M5's racing heritage and technology
