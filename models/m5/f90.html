Revolutionary Technology

The F90 M5 marked a revolutionary milestone in M history with the introduction of M xDrive, combining supercar performance with unprecedented all-weather capability.

M xDrive Innovation

The groundbreaking M xDrive system offers three modes: 4WD, 4WD Sport, and pure RWD, allowing drivers to choose between maximum traction and traditional M car dynamics.

Enhanced S63 V8

The revised S63 4.4L twin-turbo V8 now produces up to 625hp in Competition spec, with improved cooling and a unique oil supply system for track use.
