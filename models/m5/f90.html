M xDrive Revolution

The F90 M5 introduced BMW M's groundbreaking M xDrive all-wheel drive system, revolutionizing the M5's performance capabilities. This innovative system can be switched between AWD, AWD Sport, and pure RWD modes, offering unprecedented versatility and control. The result is a super sedan that combines maximum traction with traditional M car dynamics.

Performance Evolution

Available in standard, Competition, and exclusive CS variants, the F90 M5 redefined super sedan performance with supercar-rivaling acceleration while maintaining luxury comfort. The range-topping CS model, with its 627 horsepower, became the most powerful BMW M production car of its time, setting new benchmarks for four-door performance.
