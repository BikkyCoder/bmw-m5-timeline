Revolutionary Hybrid Technology

The G90 M5 marks a historic shift in M car philosophy, embracing electrification while maintaining the raw performance M cars are known for.

Hybrid Powertrain Innovation

Combining the new S68 V8 engine with a powerful electric motor, the G90 M5 delivers approximately 750hp of combined power and instant electric torque, enabling supercar-level acceleration with improved efficiency.

Electric Capability

With an estimated 50-mile electric range, the G90 M5 can operate as a pure EV for daily commuting while maintaining its high-performance capabilities for enthusiast driving.
