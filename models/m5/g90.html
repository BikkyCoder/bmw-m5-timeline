Hybrid Innovation

The G90 M5 marks a revolutionary milestone in M5 history with the introduction of hybrid technology. By combining a powerful twin-turbocharged V8 with an advanced electric motor system, it delivers unprecedented power while offering zero-emission capability for urban driving. This innovative approach sets new standards for performance and efficiency in the super sedan segment.

Modern Technology

The latest M5 showcases BMW's most advanced technology, featuring state-of-the-art driver assistance systems, the latest iDrive interface, and an enhanced M xDrive system with sophisticated hybrid integration. This new generation combines the raw performance M cars are known for with luxury amenities and sustainable mobility in a way never before seen in an M5.
