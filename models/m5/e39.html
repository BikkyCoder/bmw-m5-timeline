BMW E39 M5

The V8 Revolution

1998-2003

Technical Specifications

Engine

4.9L V8 (S62B50)

  • Double VANOS valve timing
  • 8 individual throttle bodies
  • 32-valve DOHC
  • 11.0:1 compression ratio

Performance

Power 400 hp @ 6,600 rpm
Torque 500 Nm @ 3,800 rpm
0-60 mph 4.8 seconds
Top Speed 155 mph (limited)

Transmission

6-speed Manual (Getrag 420G)

  • Heavy-duty clutch
  • 25% locking differential
  • Sport-tuned ratios

Suspension

M Sport Suspension

  • Lower ride height (-15mm)
  • Firmer springs and dampers
  • Thicker anti-roll bars

Brakes

High-Performance System

  • Front: 345mm floating rotors
  • Rear: 328mm rotors
  • ABS and DSC

Dimensions

1,795 kg (3,957 lbs)

  • Length: 4,783 mm
  • Width: 1,801 mm
  • Height: 1,412 mm

Wheels & Tires

M Parallel Spoke Design

  • Front: 8.0J x 18
  • Rear: 9.5J x 18
  • 245/40ZR18 front tires
  • 275/35ZR18 rear tires

Production

20,482 Units (1998-2003)

  • First V8-powered M5
  • Most successful M5 of its era
  • Sedan only production

* Often considered the greatest M5 ever made, featuring the first V8 engine in M5 history

The Perfect M5

The E39 M5's naturally-aspirated V8, precise 6-speed manual transmission, and perfectly balanced chassis created what many consider the greatest sport sedan ever made. Its S62 V8 engine featured individual throttle bodies, double VANOS variable valve timing, and an 8 individual coil ignition system, delivering 400 horsepower of pure mechanical excellence.

Timeless Legacy

Representing the sweet spot in M5 history, the E39 M5 combines modern reliability with pure, analog driving dynamics. Its driver-focused nature, with hydraulic steering and a manual transmission, created an engaging experience that later turbocharged models couldn't quite replicate. With 20,482 units produced, it has become one of the most sought-after modern classics.

Technical Excellence

The S62 V8 engine featured individual throttle bodies, double VANOS variable valve timing, and an 8 individual coil ignition system. Combined with its perfectly balanced chassis and driver-focused interior, the E39 M5 set new standards for performance sedans and remains highly sought after by enthusiasts.

Legacy

The E39 M5 represents the sweet spot in M5 history, combining modern reliability and performance with pure, analog driving dynamics. Its naturally-aspirated V8, manual transmission, and hydraulic steering create an engaging driving experience that later turbocharged models couldn't quite replicate.

The E39 M5 is considered by many enthusiasts to be the greatest M5 ever made, and values continue to rise as collectors seek well-preserved examples of this modern classic.
