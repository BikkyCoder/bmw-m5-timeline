Evolution & Innovations

The E34 M5 represented a significant evolution of BMW's super sedan concept, introducing numerous technological advances while maintaining the hand-built tradition of its predecessor.

Engine Evolution

Initially launched with the 3.6L S38 engine (311 hp), the E34 M5 later received an upgraded 3.8L version producing 340 hp, making it the most powerful M5 of its era.

First M5 Touring

The E34 generation introduced the first-ever M5 Touring (wagon), with only 891 units produced, making it one of the rarest and most sought-after M vehicles.
