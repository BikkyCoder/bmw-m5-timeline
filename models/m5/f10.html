Revolutionary Technology

The F10 M5 marked a paradigm shift for BMW M, embracing turbocharging and advanced electronics to create a new breed of super sedan.

Twin-Turbo Innovation

The S63 4.4L V8 with twin-scroll turbochargers delivered 560hp and massive 502 lb-ft torque from just 1,500 RPM - a 30% improvement in fuel efficiency while significantly increasing power.

M-DCT Excellence

The 7-speed M-DCT transmission offered lightning-quick 120-millisecond shifts and multiple shift programs, while US markets retained a 6-speed manual option for purists.
