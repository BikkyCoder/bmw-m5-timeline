The Turbo Revolution

The F10 M5 marked BMW M's historic shift to forced induction, with a twin-turbocharged V8 replacing the E60's naturally aspirated V10. This revolutionary change delivered massive torque across the rev range and improved fuel efficiency while maintaining the characteristic M car performance and sound.

Advanced Technology

The F10 M5 showcased BMW's latest performance technology, featuring the Active M Differential, adjustable steering weight, and customizable drivetrain settings. The first M-specific head-up display enhanced the driving experience, while the Competition Package took performance even further with increased power and sharpened dynamics.
