Formula 1 Technology

The E60 M5's naturally aspirated V10 engine was inspired by BMW's Formula 1 program. With individual throttle bodies and a stratospheric 8,250 rpm redline, it brought race car technology to the street. The engine was paired with the sophisticated SMG III transmission, with a manual option for North American enthusiasts.

Innovative Features

Beyond its revolutionary powertrain, the E60 M5 featured advanced electronics including Electronic Damper Control (EDC), dynamic stability control, and multiple engine power modes. The distinctive Bangle-era design has aged into a timeless expression of performance and sophistication.
