Revolutionary Technology

The E60 M5 marked a revolutionary leap in M car history, introducing Formula 1-derived technology and unprecedented levels of performance customization.

Formula 1-Inspired V10

The S85 V10 engine was developed using BMW's Formula 1 expertise, featuring individual throttle bodies, 8250 RPM redline, and 100hp/liter specific output - unprecedented for a production sedan.

SMG III Transmission

The 7-speed SMG III transmission offered 11 shift programs and launch control, capable of 65-millisecond shift times - a technological marvel for its era.
