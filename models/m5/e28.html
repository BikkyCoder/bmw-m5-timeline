Historical Significance

The E28 M5 marked BMW's first venture into the super sedan segment, establishing a legacy that continues to this day. Hand-built by BMW M Division's skilled technicians, each E28 M5 represented the perfect fusion of luxury and performance.

Birth of a Legend

Introduced in 1984, the E28 M5 was the fastest production sedan of its time, combining the practicality of a 5 Series with the heart of the legendary BMW M1 supercar.

Hand-Crafted Excellence

Each E28 M5 was meticulously assembled by hand at BMW M GmbH in Garching, making it one of the most exclusive BMW models ever produced.

Regional Market Information

India Market

E28 M5s are extremely rare in India, with very few examples imported. Due to their age, import regulations, and left-hand drive configuration, finding one for sale in India is challenging. Consider importing from Europe or UK markets.

South Africa Market

Limited number of E28 M5s were officially sold in South Africa. Right-hand drive examples occasionally appear on the market, typically priced between R800,000 - R1,500,000 depending on condition.

UK Market

The UK market offers some of the best availability for right-hand drive E28 M5s. Prices range from £35,000 to £85,000, with exceptional examples commanding higher prices.

Investment & Collectibility

The E28 M5 represents one of BMW's most significant collector cars, with strong appreciation potential due to:

  • ✓ Limited production (2,241 units worldwide)
  • ✓ First-generation M5 status
  • ✓ Hand-built at BMW M GmbH
  • ✓ M1-derived S38 engine
  • ✓ Growing classic car market demand
  • ✓ Historical significance
Buyer's Tip: When considering an E28 M5, prioritize service history, originality, and rust-free examples. Professional pre-purchase inspection is essential.
